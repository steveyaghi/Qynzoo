## Reference Number Format

Use this format for consistency:
```
[YYYYMMDD-###]

Example: 20251020-001
- 2025 = Year
- 10 = Month
- 20 = Day
- 001 = Sequential number for that day
```

Log this in the tracking spreadsheet!
