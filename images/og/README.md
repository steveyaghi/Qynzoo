## Design Guidelines

- **Dimensions:** 1200 × 630 px (Twitter/X minimum: 800 × 418 px)
- **Format:** PNG or JPG (not SVG — SVG is not reliably rendered by social platforms)
- **Text:** Include the article title + Qynzoo logo (bottom-right)
- **Background:** Use the site's dark gradient (#0a0f1e → #0d1b2a) to stay on-brand
- **Safe zone:** Keep text/logo within the inner 1040 × 530 px area
