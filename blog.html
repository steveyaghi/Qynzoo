How Agentic AI Works

Agentic AI follows a sophisticated 5-step process enabling autonomous decision-making:

1. Goal Setting

Define objectives and success criteria

2. Analysis

Gather and process relevant data

3. Decision

Evaluate options and choose actions

4. Execution

Implement decisions autonomously

5. Learning

Adapt and improve from outcomes
