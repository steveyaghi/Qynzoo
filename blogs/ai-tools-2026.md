The AI tools market has never been more crowded — or more confusing. After spending hundreds of hours testing and building with AI tools, here's an honest assessment of what actually works in a real business context.

## AI Thinking & Writing Tools

### Claude (Anthropic) — Top Pick
Claude excels at reasoning through problems — nuanced analysis, long document processing (up to 200,000 tokens), and producing outputs that sound like they were written by a human who understands the subject.
- **Best for:** Writing, analysis, long documents, automation via API
- **Pricing:** Free / $20/mo Pro / API pay-per-token

### Perplexity AI — Top Pick
Unlike ChatGPT or Claude, Perplexity searches the web in real time and provides cited, current answers. For competitor analysis, market sizing, and finding recent statistics, it replaces hours of manual searching.
- **Best for:** Research, fact-checking, market intelligence
- **Pricing:** Free / $20/mo Pro

## Automation Platforms

### Make (formerly Integromat) — Top Pick
Visual canvas interface, handles complex workflows with branching logic, 1,800+ integrations. Qynzoo's default recommendation for most SMEs.
- **Best for:** Most SMEs, visual workflow building
- **Pricing:** Free / €9/mo Core

### n8n — Highly Recommended
Open-source, self-hostable, dramatically cheaper at scale. Best for businesses with technical capability or data privacy requirements.
- **Best for:** Technical teams, data-sensitive businesses, high volumes
- **Pricing:** Free (self-hosted) / $20/mo cloud

## AI Customer Service

### Intercom (with Fin AI) — Highly Recommended
Fin AI agent handles customer enquiries by reading your help documentation. Can resolve 50–70% of enquiries without human intervention.
- **Best for:** SaaS businesses, e-commerce, service companies
- **Pricing:** $39/mo + $0.99/resolution

## AI Content & Knowledge

### Notion AI — Recommended
Summarises meeting notes, drafts SOPs, extracts action items. Power comes from integration with your existing workspace.
- **Best for:** Documentation, meeting summaries, SOPs
- **Pricing:** $8/mo add-on

### Midjourney — Specialist Tool
Generates professional-quality images from text descriptions. Cost-effective for marketing visuals and social content.
- **Best for:** Marketing visuals, social content, concept imagery
- **Pricing:** $10/mo Basic
